ABOUT


For this project I worked with C# making a CRUD application allowing also to retrive account by sending an email and printing lists and results. University's face or the face of the university is an application intended for professors, administration and students allowing them to access their university accounts either to insert notes (for professor), manage privileges of accounts (for administration), or to consult the results (for students)
